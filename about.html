About Us

We are a Yoga Center located in the Gracia neighborhood of Barcelona. We offer in-person and online YOGA classes, both group and private sessions, as well as workshops and retreats in nature.

We embrace the diversity of yoga from a non-dogmatic perspective, fostering a vibrant yet non-competitive environment. We value people and take pride in providing personalized guidance to our students.

Our approach is rooted in tradition while enriched by updated knowledge in physiology, biomechanics, and psychology, ensuring that yoga practice is both beneficial and healthy without losing its essence.

Our mission is to provide personalized, up-to-date, and high-quality teaching. Whether as a practitioner seeking a deeper understanding of yoga or as a teacher aiming to build a solid foundation, specialize in various areas, and stay updated, we are here to support your journey.
